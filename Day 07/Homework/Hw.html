About Georgian war in 1121

In 1121, at the Battle of Didgori, King David IV of
Georgian defeated the seljuk army. The battle
happened on the Didgori Field, West of Tbilisi.
Because of David IV's smart plan and trick, the
Seljuk army was destroyed, and the Georgians
won an important victory. This event made it
possible to take control of Tbilisi and start
Georgia's Golden Age.
